# Provider Strategy

## Sora

Use for:

- premium cinematic hero shots
- polished product visuals
- high-finish brand moments

## Kling

Use for:

- controlled motion
- character-driven shots
- lip-sync-friendly or action-oriented beats

## Higgsfield

Use for:

- exploratory cinematic concepts
- surreal or experimental worldbuilding
- early-stage creative exploration

## Integration rule

The UI never talks directly to provider APIs. Provider request shape, credentials, and future job normalization remain behind server-side adapters.
